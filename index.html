"Wassaby" cover-band website

"Wassaby" is musical group from Tomsk. It was founded by Ivan Lukiev in 2017 during his first year at Tomsk Polytechnic University. The group's repertoire is varied, but one thing has remained constant - the frantic energy at their concerts.
